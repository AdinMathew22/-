Our Manifesto

The right to self-expression knows no medical diagnosis.

For too long, medical devices have been designed as invisible burdens — clinical, institutional, and stripped of the individuality of the person wearing them. At אפולו, we reject that paradigm entirely.

We believe that a prosthetic limb can be a statement piece. That a wheelchair can be an extension of your personal aesthetic. That an insulin pump can be as considered as the watch on your wrist. That every device you depend on should make you feel powerful, not diminished.

Every אפולו creation is engineered to the highest medical standards while being designed with the craft and intention of luxury fashion. Because you deserve both.

01
Form & Function
Uncompromising clinical performance married to intentional, beautiful design. Never one without the other.
02
Radical Dignity
Your device should make you stand taller. We design for confidence, pride, and unapologetic self-expression.
03
Deep Craft
Aerospace-grade materials, master artisans, and meticulous finishing — the same care given to any luxury object.
04
Bespoke First
Your body, your story, your style. Every creation can be fully customised to your specifications and identity.

The אפולו Standard

Every detail, considered.

I
⚕️

Clinical Excellence

Every device is CE marked and FDA cleared where applicable. Designed in collaboration with leading rehabilitation specialists, prosthetists, and occupational therapists. Beauty never comes at the cost of function.

II
✦

Atelier Craftsmanship

Each piece is individually handcrafted in our Tel Aviv atelier. Master artisans with backgrounds spanning jewellery, aerospace engineering, and fine leatherwork bring your device to life.

III
🪞

Identity-Led Design

We begin every relationship with a deep conversation about who you are — your aesthetic, your lifestyle, your story. Your device should be an extension of your identity, not a departure from it.

White-Glove Service

The Bespoke Journey

A fully bespoke אפולו device is a collaboration between you and our atelier team — an intimate process that results in something made entirely for you, and no one else.

  • 01
    Consultation
    A private session with your dedicated אפולו advisor and clinical specialist. We learn your needs, lifestyle, and vision — nothing is assumed.
  • 02
    Design Studio
    Our designers present material samples, finish options, and concept sketches. You collaborate, refine, and approve every detail.
  • 03
    Atelier Creation
    Master craftspeople build your device by hand. You receive photographic updates throughout the 6–12 week creation process.
  • 04
    Fitting & Delivery
    A white-glove fitting appointment, clinical calibration, and lifetime aftercare. Your device, perfected.
